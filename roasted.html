Roasted Tomato Soup

Roasted tomato soup is a flavorful and comforting dish made by roasting fresh tomatoes to enhance their natural sweetness and depth of flavor.
The roasted tomatoes are then blended into a smooth and rich soup, often combined with other vegetables, herbs, and seasonings.

prep time

15 min.

cook time

5 min.

serving

4 servings

Tags : Breakfast Appetizer Main Drinks

instructions

step 1

                Marinate the Meat:

In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, olive oil, orange juice, lime juice, minced garlic, jalape√±o (if using), cumin, chili powder, smoked paprika, dried oregano, salt, and pepper.
Place the steak in a shallow dish or a large resealable plastic bag.
Pour the marinade over the steak, making sure it's well coated.
Marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1-2 hours, or preferably overnight for more flavor.
Preheat the Grill:

Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
Grill the Meat:

Remove the steak from the marinade and let it come to room temperature for about 15-30 minutes.
Grill the steak for 4-6 minutes per side, or until it reaches your desired level of doneness. Cooking times may vary based on the thickness of the steak and your grill's temperature.
Rest and Slice:

Once the steak is done, transfer it to a cutting board and let it rest for a few minutes to allow the juices to redistribute.
Slice the meat against the grain into thin strips.

step 2

                
                Serve:

Arrange the sliced Carne Asada on a serving platter.
Garnish with chopped cilantro and serve with lime wedges on the side.

step 3

                Optional Serving Suggestions:

Serve the Carne Asada with warm tortillas for tacos or with rice and beans.
You can also add grilled vegetables like bell peppers and onions for fajitas.
